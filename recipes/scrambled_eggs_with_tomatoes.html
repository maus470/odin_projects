Scrambled eggs with tomatoes

TIRED OF YOUR REGULAR SCRAMBLED EGGS? WELL, HOW ABOUT YOU TRY THIS BEAST OF A SCRAMBLED EGGS WITH TOMATOES RECIPE! THIS RECIPE WILL HELP YOU REGAIN FOCUS AS WELL AS IT WILL HELP YOUR BODY REGENERATE AS IT'S RICH IN ANTYOXIDANTS AND MANY NUTRIENTS

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 medium sized tomatoe
  • spices: salt, pepper, powdered garlic

steps

  1. Dice your tomatoe
  2. add your diced tomatoes onto a pan with any grease of your liking
  3. sprinkle the tomatoes with salt, pepper and powdered garlic
  4. fry on high heat until they break down into a dense liquid like substance
  5. reduce to low heat and add your eggs, spriknle them with a good amount of salt
  6. now onto high heat and scramble them until the meal is ready
